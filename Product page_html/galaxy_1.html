Samsung Galaxy S20 128GB Gray 4G


Product description

The Samsung Galaxy S20 128GB 4G Gray has 128GB memory. This is enough for 60,000 photos, for example. You can view these photos on the 6.2-inch Quad HD 120Hz screen. Thanks to the refresh rate of 120 times per second, you can scroll through your timeline smoothly. In addition to the standard lens, you can also find a wide-angle lens and a telephoto lens on the back of the device. Thanks to the powerful Samsung Exynos 990 processor and 8GB RAM, the Samsung S20 can handle the most demanding tasks. You can switch between different apps smoothly and start demanding games without lags. This device also has a 4000mAh battery, which will last all day. The S20 also supports Power Delivery, so you can quickly charge the battery.
