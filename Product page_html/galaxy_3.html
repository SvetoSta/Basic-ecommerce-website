Samsung Galaxy A51 128GB White


Product description

The Samsung Galaxy A51 128GB White combines good cameras with an excellent screen and battery. The 128GB storage offers enough room for your movie and music collection. You can use the 48-megapixel standard lens during the day and at night to take sharp photos. The wide-angle lens allows you to capture monumental buildings and panoramic panoramas. If you want to capture the smallest details of a flower or jewelry, you can use the macro lens. Once you've taken a photo, you can check ik out on spacious 6.5-inch screen. You can also stream your favorite series on the go, because the large 4000mAh battery ensures the Samsung Galaxy A51 last longer than a day on a single charge. Thanks to the 4GB RAM and mid-range processor, the A51 is fairly smooth. You won't have the speed and computing power of the Samsung A71, for example, but you'll only notice that when you run demanding apps or games.
