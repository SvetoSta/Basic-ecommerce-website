Huawei P30 Lite 128 GB Blue


Product description

On the 128GB storage memory of the Huawei P30 Lite 128GB Blue, you have enough space for your apps and files. The Huawei P30 Lite is a mid-range smartphone with a good battery life and a triple camera. In addition to the 48-megapixel camera and a 2-megapixel depth sensor, the device also has an 8-megapixel camera with a wide-angle lens. This lets you capture a larger area than with a standard lens, which is ideal for vast landscapes and tall buildings. Huawei's camera AI adjusts the camera settings automatically, so you can effortlessly take photos. With 128GB of storage, there's more than enough space to store all these photos. The 6.1-inch Full HD screen has thin bezels and a small notch containing the selfie camera. With this 24-megapixel camera, you can sharply capture yourself and your friends. What's also useful is that the P30 Lite recharges quickly, thanks to the quick charge function.
