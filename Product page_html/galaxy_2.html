Samsung Galaxy S10 128GB Blue


Product description

The Samsung Galaxy S10 Blue has 128GB storage memory, enough for 320 episodes of your favorite series or more than 60,000 photos. The device doesn't have a notch, but a small hole in the screen with the selfie camera behind it. On top of that, the S10 has thin bezels, so you can optimally enjoy the sharp 6.1-inch Quad HD screen. This screen also has the fingerprint scanner built into it. With 3 rear cameras, you'll have the right camera for any situation. Capture a large group of people or a vast landscape with the ultra wide-angle lens. Use the telephoto lens to zoom in on distant objects. The optic image stabilization makes sure your pictures aren't blurry. With Samsung's powerful Exynos 9820 processor, you'll have no problem with demanding apps, and your phone will always run smoothly.
