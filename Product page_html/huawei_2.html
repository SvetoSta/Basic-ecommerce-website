Huawei Nova 5T Black


Product description

The Huawei Nova 5T 128GB Black is a fast, mid-range smartphone with a large 6.26-inch screen and 4 rear cameras. The wide-angle lens is suitable for landscape photography or group photos and you can use the macro lens for detailed close-ups or photos of small objects. On top of that, you can take razor-sharp selfies thanks to the 32-megapixel camera. The large 6.26-inch Full HD screen provides enough space for movies, series, or games. Thanks to 6GB RAM and the powerful Kirin 980 Octa-Core processor, you can smoothly run demanding apps and easily switch between different apps. The smartphone easily lasts all day on a single charge. Forgot to charge it? Thanks to the quick charge function, you can quickly charge the smartphone's 3750mAh battery.
