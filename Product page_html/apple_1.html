Apple iPhone 11 128GB Green


Product description

The 6.1-inch Liquid Retina Display of the Apple iPhone 11 128GB Green is just as large as that of the iPhone Xr, but this device has a lot of new features. Thanks to the wide-angle lens, you can photograph large groups of people or vast landscapes. Thanks to Smart HDR, the screen displays all colors extremely vibrantly. When you're recording, you can easily switch between the standard lens and the wide-angle lens. With the 12-megapixel selfie camera, you can now also take selfies in slow motion. Thanks to the powerful A13 processor, everything runs smoothly and quickly. This energy-efficient chip makes sure the battery lasts one hour longer than that of the iPhone Xr. Unlocking your device with FaceID is even faster and works with wider angles compared to the older models. The iPhone 11 Green is waterproof and its glass screen isn't very susceptible to scratches, which means you can take your smartphone with you no matter where you go.
