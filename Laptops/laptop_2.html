Acer Extensa 15 EX215-51-57MY


Product description

The Acer Extensa 15 EX215-51-57MY is a laptop for business users who multitask or make digital designs in Photoshop. Thanks to the Intel Core i5 processor and 8GB RAM, you can work with multiple demanding programs at the same time. When you're working at home or at a different location, you can connect to your computer via Windows 10 Pro. You can also do this out in the sun, because the anti-glare screen ensures you won't be bothered by reflections of the bright light. No WiFi near you? You can connect an Internet cable to the Ethernet port for fast Internet.


Our expert recommends this laptop for:
  • Sending emails, working with Office programs and management systems
  • Digital design with programs like Adobe Photoshop
This laptop isn't suitable for:
  • Editing company films with programs like Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Running virtual environments in programs like VMware
  • Rendering 3D objects in programs like AutoCAD
