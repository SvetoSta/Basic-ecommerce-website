MSI GP65 Leopard 9SD-021NL


Product description

The MSI GP65 Leopard 9SD-021NL is a laptop that you can use to play games such as Fortnite on ultra settings. Thanks to the NVIDIA GTX 1660Ti video card, you can also get started with VR games. The 144Hz screen smoothly displays fast action, which can come in handy when playing fast shooters. With the i7 processor and 16GB of RAM, you can run programs such as Discord and Skype on the background to provide your gaming buddies with tactical instructions. Thanks to the 256GB SSD, Windows starts quickly, while the 1TB hard drive can store all of your games. The RGB lighting on the keyboard provides cool colors in the dark.
